About StockLeague

Learn how to trade stocks risk-free with a competitive community

What is StockLeague?

StockLeague is a competitive paper trading platform where you can practice trading stocks with virtual money in a risk-free environment. Compete with friends, join leagues, and climb the leaderboard to become the top trader!

Perfect for: Beginners learning to trade, students, seasoned investors practicing strategies, or anyone wanting to compete in a fun, risk-free environment.

Key Features

Real-Time Stock Data

Live quotes powered by Yahoo Finance API

Interactive Charts

30-day price history with Chart.js visualization

Portfolio Management

Track your investments with detailed metrics

Global Leaderboard

Compete with traders worldwide in real-time

Transaction History

Complete record of all your trades and performance

Market Overview

Track trending stocks and market movements at a glance

Getting Started in 5 Steps

  1. Register for a free account with $10,000 virtual cash
  2. Research stocks using our quote tool with detailed charts
  3. Trade by buying and selling stocks without any risk
  4. Track your portfolio performance, gains, and losses in real-time
  5. Compete on the global leaderboard and climb the ranks
