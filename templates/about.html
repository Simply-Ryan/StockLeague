About StockLeague

What is StockLeague?

StockLeague is a competitive paper trading platform where you can practice trading stocks with virtual money in a risk-free environment. Compete with friends, join leagues, and climb the leaderboard to become the top trader!

Key Features

  • Real-Time Stock Data: Live quotes powered by Yahoo Finance API
  • Interactive Charts: 30-day price history with Chart.js visualization
  • Portfolio Management: Track your investments with detailed metrics
  • Leaderboard: Compete with other traders globally
  • Transaction History: Complete record of all your trades
  • Market Overview: Track popular stocks at a glance

Getting Started

  1. Register for a free account with $10,000 virtual cash
  2. Research stocks using our quote tool with charts
  3. Trade by buying and selling stocks
  4. Track your portfolio performance and gain/loss
  5. Compete on the leaderboard with other traders

Technology Stack

Backend
  • Python 3.13
  • Flask 3.0
  • SQLite Database
  • Yahoo Finance API
Frontend
  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • Bootstrap 5
  • Chart.js
  • Font Awesome
